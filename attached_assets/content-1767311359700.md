## Data Aggregation

Airwaves OS seamlessly feeds your decoded aviation and marine data to multiple aggregation platforms. For ACARS, VDL, HFDL, SATCOM, and AIS, we support [Airframes](https://airframes.io/), the world's leading open aggregator for these data types.

For ADS-B data, Airwaves OS integrates with popular aggregators like FlightAware, ADS-B Exchange, and FlightRadar24, allowing you to contribute to the global aviation tracking network.

- Multiple aggregator support for various data types
- Automatic, privacy-respecting data sharing
- Enables global coverage and redundancy
- Drives open research and transparency

[Feed Now→](https://app.airframes.io/about)
